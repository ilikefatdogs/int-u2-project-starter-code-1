Fading Nights

Why care?

Light Pollution not only affects you but also our enviorment and the animals living in it. There are 3 types of light pollution, skyglow, glare, and trespass. Light pollution contributes to climate change and wastes immense amounts of energy. According to the National Park Service the night skys brightness increased by 9.6% each year from 2011-2022.

How you can help!

  1. LED and CFL lights reduce energy waste.
  2. Using warm colored light bulbs
  3. Use shields that protect outside lights from the sky
  4. Turn off lights that arent being used
  5. Reduce blue light use during the night
